WELCOME TO OUR CAMPUS

Welcome to the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Berhampur, established in 2016 under the umbrella of the Indian government's Ministry of Education (formerly known as the Ministry of Human Resources). Renowned for its commitment to global excellence, we are a hub of transformative education, groundbreaking research, and more.

IISER, being a distinguished scientific institution in India, boasts multiple campuses throughout the nation, primarily in urban areas. However, IISER Berhampur campus introduces a fresh paradigm for IISERs. Situated in an exceptionally remote setting, enveloped solely by a handful of fishing villages, it remains untouched and flourishes with a rich ecosystem of diverse flora and fauna.
